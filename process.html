Our Process, Our Strength.

Process

To have a successful completion of projects a major role is held by the process that is followed in the project. AJ interiors opts an approach for the process which has been developed by the experts that hold a lot of experience in the field of interior design and project management. Our process has always proved it worth and has always helped us delivering the projects and meeting our deadlines without fail.

UNDERSTANDING OUR CLIENTS

We begin with interior designing process by first having a chat/call with you - it’s a casual approach, where we look to understand your needs, preferences, way of style and overall vision for how you want your home/ office to be. It’s also a point for us to discuss all the options you have to work with. From furniture and lighting to colours and space, every single detail is dwelt on to understand you as a person or a family looking for an outstanding home.

SKETCHES & DEVELOPMENT

This is when we visit your home - our goal being to get a feel of the place. Available areas are measured. The space is planned, sketched and shown to you. We discuss the whole thing with you. like where does the furniture go? What approach on walls, How kitchen is going to look like, and many more will be addressed. From furniture and lighting to colours and space, every single detail will be shown to understand you looking for an outstanding home.

DRAWINGS & ESTIMATION

After understanding all the details, design, material & rawings of house we share estimate &timelines to execute work

SITE INTERIOR EXECUTION

Project-execution experts coming together to execute work like civil, furniture, electrical with our team & supervisor & even we visit site on routine check everything that was planned and designfor you.

PROJECT HANDOVER

At the time of handover we check and sign-off on everything that was planned and manufactured just for you. This is where you see your dream home coming together.  As interior designers, we’re committed to our job which is to focus on our moto “Our Client should be Happy” the pleasure of which is all yours.
