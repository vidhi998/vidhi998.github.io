Services

We at AJ designed Interiors have a team of professional of various domains and are capable of providing expert advice and services in various domains of Interior design and architecture. We make sure that the services provide by us are of topnotch quality and are done with perfection.

TURNKEY INTERIORS SOLUTIONS

Under this vertical - turnkey interior solutions - a client or a prospective client gets to gain leverage from our seasoned approach to interior designing. Here, as one amongst the top ten interior designing companies in Bangalore we execute and deliver entire projects for you, from every small detail to your largest concern – everything is worked out.

SPACE CONSULTATION

There’s a lot anyone can do with a certain amount of given space. But will it look good? And will it work good? These are the most important questions to ask and this is where, we being experts at what we do, come into the picture. Acting as space consultants - no, not outer space - but living spaces - we help you, the client, get the most out of the living spaces you have at your disposal. Your space can be big or small, it doesn't really matter, just talk to us to utilize it effectively.

3D VISUALIZATION

Imagine this. You're sitting in our office and talking to us about how you want your home to look. A few days later, we meet and we show you how your home can look! Yes - all of this is possible. With virtual tools and patented technology, 3D-visualizations of your home, basis your plans, tastes and budgets are easily possible. But essentially, how does this help? Well, it saves us all the time, effort and money of actually putting an idea into practice without actually testing it for practicality.

QUALITY CONTROL

Quality! A lot of people tend to take that for granted, perhaps even organizations that have QC mechanisms in place. But, we Sir, we’re a little different. And by different, we mean - we do not take quality for granted. It’s something that just has to be paid attention to. Adhering to this and more, every square foot of work we deliver to you is checked for quality and your convenience. In the rare event of something going unnoticed, you can always let us know and we’d gladly help fix things up.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Project management and everything that comes as a part of it - now, that’s no walk in the park. But ironically, for us - it might just be. Given that we’re the experts at what we do - beautifying homes - to plan, manage and deliver a beautiful home to you is something we enjoy doing. Having been around for as long as we have, the very challenge of successfully doing up a home is what we’d like to call… well, just another day in the office.

AFTER SALES CARE

So, yes - we definitely provide our customers with after sales services. Just like every other service we render, the way we check on a home after a project is completed matters and completely so. This is exactly why we offer an extended 5-year warranty period to our customers, the warranty period starting off from when a project was first completed. That apart, we’re always around for regular checks on work done - all you need to do is get in touch and we’ll show up to help.
